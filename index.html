WHO IS THIS? A MYTH, CRAZY GOOD DANCER, OR AWESOME FULL STACK PROGRAMMER?

Thankfully Christina is only two of these. Christina has been programming for a little over 3 months. I would describe Christina as a professional programmer who is very passionate about her work.


But that's enough about me in third person. When I was younger, I fully intended to become a Special Agent in the FBI. Fortunately my mom told me that that specific profession might be a tiny bit dangerous. Luckily I decided against that line of work. My first experience with programming started in my second year of college. At that time I was attending Rutgers, State University of New Jersey on their main New Brunswick campus. Their Intro to Computer Science course, which centered on Java was fantastically dense and informational but a little too theory oriented for my tastes. After that hackathon I found myself voracious for learning more programming but I was impatient to start it professionally. I decided on finishing my sophomore year at Rutgers.

Pelicula Picker

Use our math to figure out your best movie fit

This was our first Rails application as a group. Within four days our team successfully
+ Utilized Nokogiri to scrape IMDB’s “in theaters” page
+ Customized algorithm to rate movie based on user choices
+ Used TheMovieDB API to fetch backdrops from movies in the current database

Through our application it's easy for anyone to find the best movie to watch in theaters through their preferences. Our customized algorithm takes into account how important this aspect is to the user's movie experience.
